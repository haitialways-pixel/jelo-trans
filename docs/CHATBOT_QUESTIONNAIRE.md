# Phalo Transportation — Assistant Policy Sheet (quick-fill)

**For:** ownership / management. **Time to fill:** ~15 minutes.

This is **not** a long essay form. It only asks about the **grey zones** of your service — the
things a real customer (a groom, a graduating student, a traveler with a delayed flight) actually
wonders about, and where **only you** know the answer. The assistant already handles the obvious
stuff (fleet, live prices, availability, booking). Here we just need your **policies**.

**How to fill it in:**
- Tick `[x]` the option that matches your policy. Add a short note only if needed.
- Skip anything that doesn't apply — the assistant will route those to a human (never guesses).
- One question = one decision. No repeats.
